    International career direction

    Cross-Market Career Story

    International positioning support can help review transferable experience, local conventions and connected career materials without providing immigration or legal advice.

    Professional terminology, resume formats, job titles and employer expectations may differ between countries and industries. Career materials may therefore need additional context for another market.

    Support may involve reviewing target-market language, transferable responsibilities, professional summaries, resume and profile alignment, cover-letter context and interview examples.

    Important boundary

    International career positioning is not legal, immigration, visa, tax, licensing or work-authorization advice. It cannot guarantee employment, relocation or immigration outcomes.
