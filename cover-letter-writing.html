    Relevant Letter Context

    Cover-letter support can help connect selected experience with a specific opportunity, explain professional motivation and keep the message focused.

    A useful cover letter should help the reader understand why selected experience is relevant to the role rather than reproduce every point already contained in the resume.

    Support may involve reviewing the opportunity, identifying relevant examples, clarifying professional motivation and organizing a concise opening, middle and closing.

    Important boundary

    Cover-letter support cannot guarantee a response, interview, employment offer, salary outcome or placement. Every statement must remain accurate and appropriate to the specific opportunity.
